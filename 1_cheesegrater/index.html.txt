<h1 style="text-align:center; margin-top:40px;">
    Cheese Grater Reverse Engineering
</h1>

<!-- Overview -->
<div class="section">
    <h2>Overview</h2>
    <p>
        This project involved reverse-engineering a 4-sided stainless steel cheese grater 
        and reproducing all components in CAD using precise measurements.  
        The model includes 12–15 individual parts fully dimensioned and mated into an 
        exploded assembly.
    </p>
</div>

<!-- Objective -->
<div class="section">
    <h2>Objective</h2>
    <p>
        The goal was to accurately replicate an existing physical product, 
        understand its geometry and manufacturing intent, and produce a complete set of 
        CAD-ready parts and assembly documentation.
    </p>
</div>

<!-- Skills Used -->
<div class="section skills-list">
    <h2>Skills Used</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Precise measurement & reverse-engineering techniques</li>
        <li>Part modeling (extrude, revolve, patterns, cuts)</li>
        <li>Assembly mates & exploded view animation</li>
        <li>Technical drawings & dimensioning</li>
        <li>Material selection & manufacturability awareness</li>
    </ul>
</div>

<!-- Additional Sections -->
<div class="section">
